Step 9 — Train model

Tạo file:

train.py

Code:

from ultralytics import YOLO

model = YOLO("yolov8n.pt")

model.train(
    data="dataset/data.yaml",
    epochs=100,
    imgsz=640,
    batch=16
)

Run:

python train.py
Step 10 — Kết quả model

PHASE 6 — OpenCV preprocess
Step 14 — Crop vùng số

Trước detect digit:

crop display area

Ví dụ:

crop = image[y1:y2, x1:x2]
Step 15 — Improve image
gray = cv2.cvtColor(crop, cv2.COLOR_BGR2GRAY)

blur = cv2.GaussianBlur(gray, (3,3), 0)

thresh = cv2.adaptiveThreshold(
    blur,
    255,
    cv2.ADAPTIVE_THRESH_GAUSSIAN_C,
    cv2.THRESH_BINARY,
    11,
    2
)

Accuracy sẽ tăng mạnh.
